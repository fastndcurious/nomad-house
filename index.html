HOW IT WORKS

An Agency for Nomads

We offer a range of different types of accommodations that suits your needs.
We just take a 5% commission fee for each accommodation booked.

Flexible and adapted

Nomad House offers a unique boutique-style service to help clients find their perfect home and then take care of all the arrangements for them, including liaising with landlords, adjusting lease terms, and much more.

The duration you want

Whether you're looking for:
a few weeks, or several months, or more than one year, we'll offer you the among a selection of accommodations that suits your needs.

Quality over quantity

We don't take pride in having more than a hundred houses in major European and American cities. We don't treat you as a client. Instead, we make you a part of the NomadHouse family. And as a part of it, you deserve all the respect, attention, and effort we can give you.
